Start a WhatsApp chat with anyone without saving their phone number.

Message on WhatsApp

How it works

Paste a message containing a WhatsApp number. Click on the generated link to go to the chat.

Is the number transmitted anywhere?

No, the script simply removes all unnecessary characters that are not part of the number.
